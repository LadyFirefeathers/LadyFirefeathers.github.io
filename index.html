my process

Challenge:

How do you take a site with hundreds of prepackaged trips and make it pleasant to use by age 50+ people of the western world?

  • Partnering with two of my classmates, Katie and Emerson, we first tackled this project by interviewing the age 50+ people in our networks about their travel habits. We created an infinity diagram and concluded that Trust is the key to this demographic. Generally speaking we found that the 50+ demographic is adventurous in travel but they don’t trust the internet as a whole.
  • My part of the project focused on creating trust though a visually simple and easy to use interface on the home page. I started designing with in the size confines of mobile and thinking as we age our motor-skills become less preses and hence I wanted big, definitive buttons.
